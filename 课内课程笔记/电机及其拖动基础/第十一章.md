## 三相绕线转子异步电动机转子对称起动电阻的计算
### 解析法
$\frac{R_{T_m}}{R_{T(m-1)}} = \frac{R_{T_{(m-1)}}}{R_{T(m-2)}} = ... = \frac{R_{T_2}}{R_{T1}} = \frac{R_{T_1}}{R_2} = \frac{T_1}{T_2}$  

<font color=red>$\beta = \sqrt[m]{\frac{T_N}{s_NT_1}}$</font>  
   - >一般取$T_1 = 1.7T_N$  

$\begin{cases}
R_{T1} = \beta R_2 \\
R_{T2} = \beta^2 R_2 \\
... \\
R_{Tm} = \beta^m R_2 \\
\end{cases}$
